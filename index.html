Taxation

EAS has 3 taxes:
IVA (vat) 10% but it's 0 for us since we invoice out of the country with a semi automated system.
IRE (income) 3% on the total income if you don't deduct national expenses or 10% if you deduct expenses (my fee to incorporate and to be your local director will be local expenses for example).
IDU (dividend) 8% paid only if you distribute the profits of the company
You will be not seen as an offshore incorporated business, with all the annexed consequences to invoice EU/US clients.
